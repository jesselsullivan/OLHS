Lost & Found Pets

Lost your pet?

Please complete the Lost Animal Report, save the file, and e-mail to info@oxfordpets.com as an attachment or fill out this online form. Be sure to include any distinguishing or unique markings on your animal. Please send at least one photo of the animal to help us identify your pet. In order to keep the content of this page current, please email us when your animal is found. You should also notify your veterinarian and other local animal hospitals in the area.

Found a pet?

You are always welcome to bring the stray animal to our shelter, which is open Monday through Saturday, 12-5pm. We will hold the animal on a stray hold for five days before putting him/her up for adoption. Please complete the Found Animal Report, save the file, and e-mail to info@oxfordpets.com as an attachment or fill out this online form. It will help us greatly if you could include any distinguishing or unique markings, and any pictures you are able to take.

If you have lost or found a pet, please tag us with information on our Facebook page to share and reach thousands of followers to help reunite pets with their owners. Include detailed information, contact information and a photo to help identify lost and found pets. Click the link below and like our page today!
