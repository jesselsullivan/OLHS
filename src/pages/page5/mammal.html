Adopt a Small Mammal/Exotic Animal

Small mammals and exotic animals are occasionally surrendered to the Oxford-Lafayette Humane Society. We frequently have small mammals such as hamsters, guinea pigs, and ferrets, and occasionally have exotic animals such as birds and livestock.

Found a small mammal or exotic animal you're interested in adopting? Please fill out the small mammal adoption application. Applications take 24-48 hours to process once submitted.The electronic small mammal adoption application can be saved to your desktop and sent as an attachment to info@oxfordpets.com .The PDF small mammal adoption application can be completed and faxed to (662) 236-7389. You can also complete the application online. Interested in a specific small mammal or exotic animal? We're happy to put you on our wish list and contact you once an animal of that breed is surrendered. Please e-mail Jenn at OLHSdirector@gmail.com with breed, age, and sex preferences.
