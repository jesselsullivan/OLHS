Need help finding a companion? Please e-mail our adoption counselor at OLHSadoptions@gmail.com to get the conversation started. We work very hard to match a potential adopter with a perfect canine companion.

Interested in a specific breed? We're happy to put you on our wish list and contact you once an animal of that breed is surrendered. Please e-mail Jenn at OLHSdirector@gmail.com with breed, age, and sex preferences.
